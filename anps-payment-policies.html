Transparent Terms and Conditions

  • Online payment is provided for school fees, transport fees and approved school charges only.
  • Parents must verify the student name, admission number and payable amount before payment.
  • Payment confirmation is subject to successful bank/payment gateway response and school account verification.
  • Fee receipts are issued from the school ERP after successful verification.

Customer Rights and Responsibilities

  • Parents may request payment clarification from the school office with transaction reference details.
  • Parents are responsible for entering the correct admission number and amount.
  • Duplicate or wrong payments must be reported to the school office as soon as possible.

Privacy Policy

Student, parent, staff and payment information is used only for school administration, fee accounting, communication and service delivery. The school does not sell personal data to third parties.

Payment credentials such as card, UPI PIN or banking passwords are not stored by the school ERP.

Refund Policy

  • Refunds are applicable only for duplicate payment, excess payment, or payment made against an incorrect student account after school verification.
  • Approved refunds are processed to the original payment mode or parent-provided bank account as per school and bank process.
  • Refund processing time depends on bank/payment gateway settlement timelines.

Pricing and Payment Breakdowns

Fees are calculated according to the student class, fee master, transport selection, due amount and applicable fine rules configured in the school ERP.

  • Tuition Fee
  • Transport Fee, where applicable
  • Annual or yearly fee, where applicable
  • Other Dues, where applicable
  • Fine or previous dues, where applicable

Cancellation Policy

School fee payments cannot normally be cancelled after successful bank authorization. For accidental or duplicate payments, parents must contact the school office for refund or adjustment.

Shipping Policy

No physical product is shipped. The service is digital school fee payment and school ERP access.

  • Shipping charges: Not applicable
  • Shipping methods: Not applicable
  • Domestic/international shipping: Not applicable
  • Estimated delivery time: Not applicable

Accepted Payment Methods

Payment gateway and UPI options may be enabled by the school after bank approval.

UPI Net Banking Debit Card Credit Card ICICI Gateway

Recurring Charges

There is no automatic recurring debit enabled by default. Parents pay fees manually unless the school separately enables and communicates an approved recurring payment facility.
